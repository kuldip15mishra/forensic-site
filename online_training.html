ONLINE TRAINING:-

We provide online training to the Students of Forensic Science on the subject of handwriting and fingerprints.  We guide how to prepare the technical reports in various types of handwriting and fingerprints cases in which opinion of experts are required.

FORMAT OF TRAINING:-
In this training programme, we provide the assignments of different types of Court cases to the students and give chance to solve them according to his/her ability, knowledge and skill. Thereafter, we provide soft copy of these reports through e- mail and hard copy by speed post on the corresponding address for easily understanding and demonstration. Basically before starting professional career in Forensic Science, student should have command to write report of every type of case on the subject of Handwriting and Fingerprints. After Online training, any student who is qualified in B.Sc and M.Sc Forensic science can start his/her professional career as an independent forensic expert in different courts of India. We guide-
